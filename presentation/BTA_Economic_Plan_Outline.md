### Slide 13: R40 Truck Problem
**The Challenge:**
- Trucks from Limpopo use R40 for industrial soil transport
- Illegal passenger pickups undercut taxi industry
- Road damage from heavy vehicles

**Visual:** Problem illustration with trucks and damaged roads

---

### Slide 14: R40 Solutions
**Our Three-Point Solution:**

**1. Road Usage Levy**
- Payment required for R40 use
- Enforced via permits/checkpoints

**2. Illegal Pickup Fines**
- R5000+ fines for first offense
- Escalating penalties

**3. Public Awareness Campaign**
- Community education about safety risks

**Visual:** Solution steps with icons

## Presentation Design Guidelines:

### Color Scheme:
- Primary: #1a365d (Deep blue)
- Secondary: #3182ce (Blue)
- Accent: #38a169 (Green)
- Background: #f7fafc (Light gray)

### Typography:
- Headings: Inter Bold
- Body text: Inter Regular
- Emphasis: Inter Semi-bold

### Visual Elements:
- Use consistent icons throughout
- Include charts and graphs for data
- Use high-quality images of transportation
- Maintain professional layout with plenty of white space

### Animation Suggestions:
- Slide transitions: Fade or slide from right
- Text animations: Appear on click
- Charts: Build progressively
- Keep animations professional and subtle
