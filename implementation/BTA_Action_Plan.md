## Risk Management Strategy

### Technology Risks
- **Risk:** Slow adoption of digital systems
- **Mitigation:** Comprehensive training, incentives, gradual rollout

### Financial Risks
- **Risk:** Revenue shortfall
- **Mitigation:** Diversified revenue streams, conservative projections

### Operational Risks
- **Risk:** Service quality issues
- **Mitigation:** Rigorous training, quality monitoring, feedback systems

### Regulatory Risks
- **Risk:** Policy changes
- **Mitigation:** Government partnerships, legal compliance, flexibility

### Market Risks
- **Risk:** Competition from external providers
- **Mitigation:** Community focus, first-mover advantage, continuous innovation
